They reconnected again because of an Instagram story - Victor posted a picture in an ambulance after he had a car accident. Rachel replied to the story just to check in. And they started chatting a lot more since then.
Rachel invited Victor for a drink later on to celebrate his full recovery. They talked topics over topics until late night that day. Before they said goodbye, Victor knew he could not let this girl go anymore, he kissed Rachel and asked her if she could be his girlfriend. Rachel did not say yes immediately but asked Victor to reach out again the next morning. And of course, Victor did ask her again the next day and they have been together for every ups and downs of their lives ever since.

Victor proposed to Rachel on 3 February 2022 and they decided to have their wedding day on 3 February 2024, exactly two years after the proposal.
